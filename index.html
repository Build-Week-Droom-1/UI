What is Droom?

Droom provides a way to match up with your dream job by swiping left or right. Think Tinder and LinkedIn together.

How it works

1

Create a Droom account, and check out dream job listings at a swipe of a finger.

2

Once you find your dream job and you want to apply, swipe right and begin a seamless application.

3

Lastly, sit back and relax as top-notch employers match with you.
